Fatma is holding a bachlore’s degree in Environmental Sciences and Sustainability from Zayed University and Currently postgraduate Engineering Project Management student at Abu Dhabi University. 
She is an ambition person about persevering the environment to be a better place for us and for future generations to live adds to that she has passion about Tea influenced by her dearest father Ahmad ! 
Chai by Ygnd El Ras was create by the young 19 year old Fatma in 2017 to bring the heritage art of Emirate tea to modern life by blending specialty tea leaves to create a legacy inspired by her family to bring her passion to life.
