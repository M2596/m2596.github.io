Why Ygnd ElRas?

The brand name was inspired by the father. Constantly repeating this phrase ‘Ygnd El Ras‘ while enjoying his tea set grounds to the name of the brand. Not to forget that it is also a traditional Emirati phrase, which translates to ‘mood-setter’. On that account, the purpose is to showcase an Emirate brand with an inspirational Emirate phrase.
